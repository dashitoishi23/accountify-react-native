Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22nd October 2024

Thank you for using Accountify. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data the app collects and how it is used.

1. Data Collection

This app operates entirely offline. All data, including your personal budget and expense information, is stored locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any data to external servers, third parties, or cloud services.

2. Use of Data

The data you input into the app is only accessible to you. It is used solely for the purpose of managing your budget and tracking your expenses according to the app's functionality. Since the app works offline, no data is sent over the internet.

3. Data Security

Your data remains stored on your device. We recommend that you take necessary precautions to protect your device, such as using a passcode, fingerprint, or face authentication to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Data Retention

All data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. If you uninstall the app or delete its data manually, all stored information will be permanently erased.

5. No Third-Party Sharing

As the app functions entirely offline, there is no interaction with third-party services or external platforms. Your data is never shared, sold, or transferred to any third party.

6. User Control

You have full control over the data stored in the app. You can modify or delete any information at any time within the app settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be reflected within the app. Please check this section periodically for updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us at hitoishi.das@gmail.com.
